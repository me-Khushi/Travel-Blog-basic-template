Rishikesh

Rishikesh, often called the "Yoga Capital of the World," is a serene and spiritual town located in the foothills of the Himalayas, along the banks of the sacred Ganges River in Uttarakhand, India.

Gateway of India Mumbai

The Gateway of India, one of Mumbai's most iconic landmarks, has a fascinating history and architectural grandeur. Built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to India.

Gwalior Fort

Gwalior Fort, located in the city of Gwalior (not Mumbai), is one of India's most iconic and historical forts. Its rich history, stunning architecture, and strategic importance make it a fascinating site to explore.
